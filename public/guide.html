Hightail Guide

Welcome to Hightail, your ultimate job-seeking platform designed to connect job seekers with potential employers efficiently. This guide provides an overview of the platform’s key features and functionalities to help you navigate and make the most of your job search experience.

Key Features

Job Application & Save for Later

  • Browse job listings tailored to your interests.
  • Apply for jobs instantly or save them for future applications.

Applied Jobs Section

  • Easily track the jobs you have applied for in the “Applied Jobs” section.
  • Keep a record of your job applications for better organization.

AI Chatbot – High Inta

  • Our AI-powered chatbot, High Inta, assists you with job recommendations, career advice, and general guidance.
  • Get personalized insights to help make informed career decisions.

Email-Based Communication

  • For Job Seekers: Receive a confirmation email after applying for a job.
  • For Employers: Get notified via email when a user applies for a job.
  • Employers can initiate direct communication with job seekers if their resume matches the job requirements.

Purpose & Functionality

Hightail acts as a bridge between job seekers and employers, streamlining the hiring process by:

  • Facilitating smooth and efficient job applications.
  • Enabling direct communication between job seekers and employers.
  • Automating email notifications to ensure timely updates and responses.
